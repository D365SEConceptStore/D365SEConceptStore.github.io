A Space Built for Uncommon Thinking

The Innovation Center is more than a facility — it's a curated ecosystem where human ingenuity meets state-of-the-art infrastructure. Every corner has been designed to provoke, inspire, and enable the kind of thinking that changes industries.

Whether you're a startup founder looking to test a concept, a corporate team running a design sprint, or a researcher seeking collaborative space, our center adapts to your ambitions.

  • Advanced prototyping & fabrication labs
  • Immersive presentation theatres
  • High-velocity collaboration zones
  • Executive briefing suites
  • Open ideation studios
